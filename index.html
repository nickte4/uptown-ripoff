summer fashion

Do you love to celebrate summer by finding the perfect cropped tank or sandals to complete your look? At Uptown Cheapskate, you can continue to enjoy that new summer outfit without supporting the pollution, waste, and microplastics that current fast-fashion creates. So go ahead and buy that new pair of shorts and a crochet top! Enjoy the outdoors knowing you didn’t add to the earth’s fashion pollution! No wonder the resale fashion industry is growing so quickly.

Resale is the king of sustainability. Bring in last year’s items to sell to your local Uptown Cheapskate and find some new summer fashions to enjoy this year. What a great way to love the earth and fashion! Stop in today!
